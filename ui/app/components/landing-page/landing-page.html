What is Cobudget?

Cobudget is a tool and a methodology that makes resource allocation participatory. It enables all members of an organization to get involved in decision-making by proposing projects and allocating funds to the proposals they like.

How do I start?

The Greaterthan team offers services to help you get started with Cobudget and optimize it for your needs. We cater to all types of organizations, from emerging networks, to cooperatives, to corporates.

Organizations Using Cobudget

Outlandish

Outlandish is a web services consultancy and worker-owned cooperative.

How do they use Cobudget?

To democratically distribute their dividends to everyone in the organization and make it easy for them to invest in work they care about.

What type of activities do they fund?

New tech products and platforms, social impact projects in need of software and political activism.

Pre-Cobudget, there was a lack of transparency and the authority to spend surplus was limited.
- Brian Spurling, Outlandish Collaborator

Institute for Evolutionary Leadership

IEL is a consulting company and community of thought leaders working towards a more just, sustainable, and flourishing world.

How do they use Cobudget?

To offer their member community a way to participate directly in the organization's financial decisions and empower them to fund each others workshops.

What type of activities do they fund?

Events such as workshops and trainings, travel expenses and systemic change work.

This technology is an enabler of new ways of doing things. Without it we would be using ugly spreadsheets.
- Fyodor Ovchinnikov, Co-Founder IEL
